Given 123.210.32.0/19, the base network address is 123.210.32.0 
and the broadcast address is 123.210.63.255. A total of 8192 addresses exist.

"Think" can be divided into two networks. One that is a size of 4096 and another that is 512. Since 2 addresses will already be reserved for broadcast and network addresses the total number of assinable addresses is 4092 + 512 - 4 = 4600 which is larger than the required 4500 addresses.
